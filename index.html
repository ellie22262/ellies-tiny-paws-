About Ellie's Tiny Paws

At Ellie's Tiny Paws, we specialize in raising beautiful, healthy Chihuahua and Dachshund puppies in a loving family environment. Every puppy receives proper veterinary care, early socialization, and plenty of love before joining their forever home.

Our goal is to connect wonderful puppies with caring families while providing a safe, trusted, and professional adoption experience.
